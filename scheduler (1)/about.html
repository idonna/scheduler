About Us

The Christian Development Centre, CDC is a 350-bed capacity and five-story building, located inside Tagaytay Country Homes 3 a plush subdivision in Barangay Crossing Silang East. The ministry of CDC which started in 1994 is managed by the Philippine Council of Evangelical Churches, the largest network of evangelical denominations, churches, para-church and missions organizations in the country.


CDC is the ideal venue for conferences and seminars, meetings, retreats, camps and other church related activities. CDC is also home away from home, an ideal vacation or retreat facility for pastors and ministers, individuals and families. The facility has two auditoriums, three function rooms, a spacious lobby and registration area, a lounge and a library. Objectives

  1. To make CDC a primary venue for retreats, trainings, seminars, conferences, conventions and other church-related activities.
  2. To promote CDC as a partnership of PCEC with churches in fulfilling the Great Commission.
  3. To create and sustain health awareness within the body of Christ.
  4. To post higher occupancy and profitability.
  5. To develop a higher level of Christian maturity among the workers and staff.
  6. To aim for excellence in service-oriented ministry.
  7. To foster goodwill to the community.

Goals

  1. CDC to become the first choice of PCEC member bodies as well as to non-members when looking for a venue.
  2. Introduce healthy food alternatives and influence people towards a healthier lifestyle.
  3. Minimize expenses and maximize occupancy.
  4. Teach, inspire and motivate workers/staff towards a Godly lifestyle.
  5. Continue building renovations/improvements and upgrading of equipments.
  6. Contribute to the upkeep and development of the subdivision and to reach out to the underprivileged in the community.

HOW TO GET TO CDC

  1. The Christian Development Centre (CDC), located in Tagaytay City, is an hour and fifteen minutes drive from Manila via the South Expressway or through the Coastal Road.
  2. If going through South Expressway, take Sta. Rosa Exit going straight to Tagaytay City Market turn right towards Crossing Silang Rotonda,
  3. turn right towards Manila and left to Tagaytay Country Homes 3 just after MetroBank on the right.
  4. If going by way of Coastal Road, go straight passing through Imus, Dasmarinas and Silang and turning right to Tagaytay Country Homes 3 just before the MetroBank branch.
